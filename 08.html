Vincent Valentine.

Shinra building... or what remains of it.

Shelke:

Vincent!

Why is Chaos...? Why now?

Protmateria? Control Chaos?

Chaos. While a part of you, the entity exists independently from the rest of your body.

Shelke?

Chaos. The harbinger of anarchy.

Lucrecia.
